The Panda Mission

Our mission is to save pandas — not with pity, but with power. In a world where attention and capital rule, memes and money are the sharpest weapons we’ve got. PANDA shall wield both. We will run the following initiatives:

PANDA Conservation Foundation (10%)
PANDA will lock 10% of total supply into panda charities with a 10-year vesting and a 12-month cliff. This ensures pandas get sustained protection long term.
PANDA Birth Initiative (10%)
10% of supply fuels the birth initiative. For every cub born, 1% of the remaining allocation is burned. Pandas multiply, tokens vanish, bags get heavier. Life itself makes PANDA more scarce.
Buy a Zoo, Bring Pandas Home
If a foreign zoo cages pandas, PANDA’s mission is clear: shut it down (protests or financial), and rip out the bars. Tag us with misdemeanours on our socials.
Enemies of the Pandas
PANDA will take no prisoners. Greedy zoos, shady corporations, and disgusting operators will be exposed, dragged into the spotlight on the Wall of Shame.
#FreeThePandas Campaign
Billboards, protests, memes, and chaos— PANDA will hammer cages until they break.We need warfare for attention, with one goal:free every panda.
Panda Force
PANDA will build an army that is our community.Masked pandas at zoos, conventions, television events.. Public voices. Peaceful protest in a wave so powerful that it cannot be ignored.
